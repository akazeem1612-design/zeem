# ZEEM AI - Pilot Program Agreement

## 1. Engagement
This Agreement establishes a 14-day free pilot program between Zeem AI ("Provider") and the Client for AI-powered Missed Call Recovery and Intake services.

## 2. What We Provide (Zeem AI)
- **24/7 AI Intake Engine:** Custom-trained AI receptionist capable of answering missed calls via SMS within 5 seconds.
- **Lead Qualification:** Intelligent chat flow designed to qualify leads (e.g., distinguishing service requests from DIY questions, identifying property ownership).
- **Appointment Booking:** Automated scheduling directly onto the Client’s calendar using predefined availability slots.
- **Reporting:** Daily transparency reports detailing lead volume, conversion rates, and appointment confirmations.

## 3. What You Provide (Client)
- **Call Forwarding Access:** Enabling call forwarding for missed calls to the dedicated Zeem AI Twilio number.
- **Business Logic:** Up-to-date business hours, service types offered, and calendar availability.
- **Feedback:** Prompt feedback on lead quality and AI responses during the 14-day pilot.

## 4. Success Criteria
The pilot will be considered successful if the AI successfully qualifies and attempts booking for at least **80%** of valid incoming leads (non-spam, non-sales calls) during the 14-day period.

## 5. Conversion Terms
Upon successful completion of the pilot, the Client agrees to transition to a paid agreement under the following terms:
- **Setup Fee:** $2,000 – $4,000 (one-time, based on custom integration needs).
- **Monthly Retainer:** $1,500 – $3,000/month (based on call volume and lead flow).
